Vehicle

In Fleet, you can manage Vehicles by creating an Vehicle record.

Fleet > Documents > Vehicle > New

Fill in the details of Vehicle

  • Naming Series: Select Naming Series, Default is "CAR"
  • Vehicle Name: Enter a name for Vehicle
  • Make: Enter the Make of vehicle, e.g. Ford.
  • Model: Enter Model e.g Ecosport
  • Odometer: Enter Odometer reading at the time of Purchase
  • Year: Select year from list.
  • Default Fuel: Select Item for Fuel (Tank Capacity UOM depends on this)
  • Tank Capacity: Enter Tank Capacity e.g. 60 Liters
  • Notes: Enter specific Notes
  • License Plate: Enter License Plate Number
  • VIN: Enter Vehicle Identification Number
  • Insurance Policy: Enter Insurance Policy Name
  • Date of Expiry: Enter Date of Expiry for Insurance Policy
  • Purchase Receipt: Select Purchase Receipt if any
  • Purchase Price: Enter Purchase Price
  • Purchase Date: Enter Purchase Date
  • Delivery Note: Select Delivery Note if any
  • Sales Price: Enter Sale Price
  • Sale Date: Enter Sale Date
