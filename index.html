Fleet Management App for ERPNext

Fill Up Records, Trip Records and Fleet Database integrated into ERPNext

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Fleet and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app fleet https://github.com/revant/fleet
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app fleet

Login to your site to configure the app.
